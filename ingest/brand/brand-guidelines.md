# FixIt Egypt — Brand Guidelines

> Working brand guidelines, assembled from the official logo (`logo/fixit-logo.jpeg`), the
> website mockups, and the "Content & Branding" section of the Website Brief.
> Color hex values below are **approximated from the logo artwork and should be confirmed**
> against the original vector/brand source once it is supplied.

## Logo

- **Official logo:** `logo/fixit-logo.jpeg` — a navy house outline containing a wrench, with an orange roof/flag accent and a small window. Wordmark "FixIt" with "Fix" in navy and "It" in orange, above the strapline "RELIABLE HOME SERVICES" ("RELIABLE" in orange, "HOME SERVICES" in navy).
- Always use the official supplied asset. **Do not recreate the logo in CSS or redraw it.**
- ⚠️ Only a raster JPEG is on file so far. **Request a vector (SVG/AI/EPS) and transparent-background PNG** versions for web and print use.

## Brand name

- Written **"FixIt"** (capital F, capital I, no space) — sometimes styled "FixIt Egypt".
- Tagline / strapline: **"Reliable Home Services."**
- Positioning statement: *"Go-to online marketplace for all things home services."*

## Colors (approximate — confirm against source)

| Role | Color | Approx. hex |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Primary — Navy | Deep navy blue (logo, headings, UI text, primary buttons) | ~ `#16335B` |
| Accent — Orange | Vivid orange (highlights, CTAs, "It", offers/badges) | ~ `#F47B20` |
| Neutral — White | Backgrounds | `#FFFFFF` |
| Neutral — Dark text | Body copy | ~ `#1A1A1A` |

Navy = trust/professionalism; Orange = energy/speed/action (used for CTAs like "Book on WhatsApp" and discount badges).

## Brand personality

Professional · Trustworthy · Fast · Friendly · Modern.

## Voice & tone

- Clear, confident, reassuring — sells trust and reliability over hype.
- Customer-facing and approachable; emphasizes speed and transparency.
- **Bilingual: every customer-facing element must exist in both English and Arabic.**

## Key messaging pillars

- Fast response
- Verified technicians
- Transparent pricing
- Reliable service

## Design direction (from brief)

- Clean modern UI, simple navigation.
- High-trust visuals (real technician photos, verified badges, before/after).
- Strong, obvious CTA buttons.
- Mobile-first UX.
